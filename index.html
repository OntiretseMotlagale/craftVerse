Welcome to Craftverse

Craftverse is an online community for cosplayers and crafters to exchange secrets, make a name for themselves, and explore business opportunities.

Engage in the
community

Out mission at Craftverse is to cosplayers professionals and enthusiast alike a space to showcase their craft, inspire and explore lucrative opportunities, and unlock their potential in a safe industry-friendly environment.

Express Yourself

Craftverse is a cosplay for enthusiast. Gone are the days of fragmented online cosplay groups. Together we will create a welcoming environment where you can share your latest creations and projects.

Grow Your Knowledge

What's your goal ? Blacksmithing ? 3D Printing ? Painting ? from sowing to foam work, we'll work with industry leaders to help you learn the latest cosplay secrets and techniques.

Have Fun.
Make Money.

Craftverse empowers you to sell digital and physical products to the worldwide cosplay community. Together we will connect, makers, brands, and events to the vast world of cosplay and crafting.
